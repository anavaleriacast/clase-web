dir. Laura Amelia Guzmán e Israel Cárdenas

Cisco tiene la espalda en contra las cuerdas. Luego de haber pasado 15 años en una prisión americana, regresa a la República Dominicana sin trabajo; otro problema que se suma a los habitos delinctivos de su hermano menor...Nichi, un ex-boxeador italiano, ve la promesa financiera detrás de la rudeza callejera de Cisco.

Cisco has his back against the ropes. After spending 15 years in an American prison, he’s returned to the Dominican Republic, unable to get a job; a problem compounded by his younger brother’s delinquent habits. But Cisco finds a possible salvation in Nichi, an Italian ex-boxer who sees dollar signs in Cisco’s gritty fighting skills.
